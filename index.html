About Me

I am Haresh Jadav, currently working as an Assistant Professor of Physics at Parul University, Vadodara. My interdisciplinary expertise spans Physics, Planetary Science, Astrophysics, Space Science, Condensed Matter Physics, and Materials Science.

My primary research focuses on Mars ionosphere, magnetosphere, solar wind interaction, space weather, planetary evolution, solar system exploration, theoretical and observational planetary studies, instrument development for space missions, AI/ML applications in space science, advanced materials development for space applications, astronomy, astrophysics, Mars surface exploration, and planetary sample analysis.

I also have strong research experience in Condensed Matter Physics and Materials Science with hands-on expertise in advanced characterization instruments including XRD, SEM, FESEM, TGA, DSC, Raman spectroscopy, UV-Vis, FTIR, Mass Spectrometry, and more.

My long-term goal is to pursue a PhD in Physics focused on Mars research and contribute to major discoveries in planetary exploration and future space missions.
